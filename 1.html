Solar

Kim Yong-sun (Korean: 김용선, born February 21, 1991), better known by the stage name Solar (Korean: 솔라), is a South Korean singer and songwriter signed under RBW. She is the leader of girl group Mamamoo. She made her solo debut with the single "Spit it Out" on April 23, 2020.
