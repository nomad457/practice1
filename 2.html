Moonbyul

Moon Byul-yi (Korean: 문별이, born December 22, 1992), better known by the mononym Moonbyul, is a South Korean rapper, singer, songwriter, dancer, and actress signed under RBW. She is the main rapper and main dancer of the South Korean girl group Mamamoo. In May 2018, Moonbyul made her debut as a solo artist, releasing the digital single, "Selfish".
