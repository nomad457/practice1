WheeIn

Jung Whee-in (born April 17, 1995), known professionally as Wheein, is a South Korean singer signed under RBW. She is best known as a member and vocalist of South Korean girl group Mamamoo. In April 2018, Wheein made her debut as a solo artist with her digital single "Easy".
